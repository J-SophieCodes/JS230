About

WITS, the only technical conference with all women speakers, is the conference for women working in and with technology. Through sessions tackling the latest trends in technology and innovating your career, WITS is on the cutting edge of topics leading the technology industry.

Join us in celebrating WITS 10th Anniversary with two virtual summits, monthly connect events, and a 10th anniversary celebration, designed to educate, inspire and connect women in tech.

All profits generated from each event support the non-profit TechGirlz.org and its ongoing mission to inspire middle school girls to explore the possibilities of technology to empower future careers.
